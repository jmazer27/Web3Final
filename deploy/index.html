Technical Features

  • Easy and powerful interface will have you printing in minutes

  • Dual high-efficiency internal LED strips give your work broad illumination for a wide angle view

  • CO2 Laser — Custom CO2 glass laser tube capable of continuous operation at 10,600 nm; rated at 45 watts for Pro and 40 watts for Plus and Basic

  • Linear System — Custom extruded aluminum linear rails with belt-driven v-wheel carrier

  • Air Assist — Internal air assist with no external compressor hookup required

Home Use

  • The Glowforge Air Filter uses activated carbon and HEPA filters to clean the air that comes out of your Glowforge printer

  • The filter sits on the floor near your printer and comes with handles and wheels so it’s easy to move

  • It has an on/off switch and a dial to adjust the fan power for maximum filter life

  • Certified to UL standards with Safety sensors that alert in case of bumps or unplanned movement, warn in case of overtemperature conditions and notify when fan performance changes
